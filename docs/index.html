Have any questions?

My research focuses on Applied Mathematics, particularly Nonlinear PDEs, Solitons, Solitary Wave Theory, and the application of Machine Learning techniques to solve complex mathematical problems.

I have published in prestigious journals including Physica Scripta, and have several manuscripts under review in journals such as International Journal of Geometric Methods in Modern Physics, Nonlinear Differential Equations and Applications NoDEA, and others.

I have extensive teaching experience as a Visiting Lecturer at multiple institutions including National University of Modern Languages, HITEC University, and International Islamic University, where I teach various Mathematics courses to undergraduate students.

I have completed several certifications including "Artificial Intelligence for everyone" from Stanford University, "Python for Data Science, AI & Development" from IBM, and "Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI)" from IBM.

I'm open to research collaborations in areas of Applied Mathematics, Nonlinear PDEs, Solitons, and Machine Learning applications. Please contact me via email with your research proposal or ideas for potential collaboration.

I am passionate about making a positive impact on youth through teaching by imparting knowledge in innovative ways. I believe in creating an engaging learning environment that fosters critical thinking and practical application of mathematical concepts.
